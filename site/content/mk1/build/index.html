Engravinator MK1 — Build

Follow along with the steps below and you should have everything you'll need to guide you through the assembly process.

The build should take about 6 to 8 hours for most people. So put on some good music, bribe a friend with good food, grab a comfy chair and dig in. We truly believe it's a fun build, just be sure to take your time and read carefully.

If you have any questions at all, head on over to the Engravinator Forum and we'll get you sorted out.
