Beta Tester Request Form

By filling out this form, you are indicating that you want to be a beta tester for the Engravinator. If you are chosen for our beta tester program, you will be sent an invoice for $375 plus shipping cost and we will send you a ready-to-assemble kit. You will be expected to assemble the kit yourself following the instructions on our site and provide feedback on the documentation and your experience with the device.

If you are local to Raleigh, NC a member of the Engravinator team can arrange to deliver your kit to you without shipping cost. If you are outside of the RTP area, we will need to ship the Engravinator to you for an additional fee. Due to various concerns around exporting laser units from outside the United States, we are only accepting beta testers located in the continental US only.

You are more than welcome to share your experience and build process with anyone (including social media), but we ask that you please do not sell or give away your Engravinator until we have gotten all the feedback from you we need.
